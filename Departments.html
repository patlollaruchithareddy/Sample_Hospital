Dermatology

Dermatology is a branch of medicine that deals with conditions associated with skin, hair, nails, and mucous membrane. The doctors who specialize in the study and diagnosis of the conditions related to the skin, hair, nails and mucus membrane are called dermatologists. Khailash Hospitals in India has a specialized department of dermatology serving all the needs of the skin and its related issues.
